Changing the World, One Beer at a Time

We developed Impossible Beer as a way to combat against the effects of climate change on the beer industry. Barley yields, one of the main ingredient in beer, are predicted to go down 15% in the next few years due to climate change. This would have caused beer shortages and an increase in beer prices. That's where we come in...

We have developed a barley-alternative to produce our beer. It grows in all climates and won't be impacted by the effects of climate change. It is also produced sustainably so we won't be adding to global warming with our practices.
