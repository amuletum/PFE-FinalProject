Changing the World, One Beer at a Time

We developed Impossible Beer as a way to combat against the effects of climate change on the beer industry. We produce our beer with a barley-alternative. It uses regenerative agriculture techniques, which means it's sustainably produced and you can enjoy your beer knowing that you're helping combat climate change.

Not only are barley yields predicted to go down due to climate change, but the beer industry is heavily pollutive and has a large carbon footprint. It takes a single tree 60 days to offset the carbon emissions from a traditional sixpack of beer.

That's where we come in...

We created Impossible Beer to show the world that it's possible to create a beer that has no carbon footprint. In fact, we create a negative footprint since we donate 5% of all proceeds to offset carbon emissions.
We offer a premium beer subscription service. Crisp, chilled beer delivered right to your door. With no carbon footprint.
